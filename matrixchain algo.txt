MCM
Algorithm..
Matrix-Multiply(A,B)
1.If columns[A]!=rows[B]
2. then error “incomplete dimensions”
3. else for i = 1 to rows[A]
4.        	do for j = 1 to columns[B]
5.                        	do C[i,j] = 0
6.                                        	for k = 1 to columns[A]
7.                                                        	Do C[i,j]=C[i,j]+A[i,k]*B[k,j]
8. Return C
